About Me

After spending twelve years in help-desk and network support and five years attending Kennesaw State University, I graduated in 2012 with a Bachelor’s Degree in English Education, I spent the last five years working as an in-car and classroom driving instructor as well as an English, language arts, and social studies teacher in Cherokee and Paulding Counties. I acquired valuable team communication, project organization, and time management skills over my years in the corporate and educational world.

I am currently a Full Stack Web Developer focusing on Back-End (MySQL, Node.js, React), Web Design (Photoshop), and Development (HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery, WordPress, Bootstrap, Responsive Layouts).

I am currently based in Atlanta, GA. My primary hobbies include board games, cooking, and reading.
